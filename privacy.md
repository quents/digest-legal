# Privacy Policy — Digest

**Last updated: April 25, 2026**

Digest ("the app", "we", "us") is an iOS application that summarizes content you share using AI. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data we collect

We collect the minimum information needed to provide the service.

### Account information
- **Apple ID email** — only if you choose to share it during Sign in with Apple
- **Display name** — optional, from Sign in with Apple
- **User ID** — an anonymous identifier we generate to link your saved items to your account

### Content you save
- **URLs, text, images, and files you share to Digest** — processed and stored so we can show you the summaries
- **AI-generated summaries** — the output of our AI processing

### Technical data
- **Push notification token** — only if you allow notifications
- **Anonymous crash reports** — diagnostic data to improve the app
- **Subscription status** — whether you have a free or pro subscription

### What we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your location
- We do not collect your contacts
- We do not track you across other apps or websites
- We do not sell your data

## How we use your data

- Process shared content with AI and return summaries to you
- Deliver push notifications when a summary is ready
- Verify your subscription status
- Diagnose and fix crashes (anonymous only)

## Third parties that process your data

To provide the service, we rely on the following categories of processors:

- **AI model providers** (currently Google Gemini) — the content you share is sent to an AI provider to generate summaries. AI providers process content according to their own data handling and retention policies.
- **Apple** — Sign in with Apple and Push Notification delivery
- **Payment and subscription management provider** — verifies subscription status
- **Cloud infrastructure, security, and error-monitoring providers** — host our servers, route requests, and collect anonymous crash reports

Each processor receives only the minimum data required to perform its function. We may change processors over time to improve the service; we will update this policy if the categories of processing change materially.

## Content rights

- Summaries are generated from content you share. Content remains owned by its original creators.
- Digest stores the source URL and summary for your personal use only.

## Data retention

- **Saved items and summaries** — kept until you delete them or delete your account
- **Crash logs** — purged after 90 days

## Your rights

- Delete individual items from within the app
- Clear your local cache in Settings > About > Clear Cache
- Delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings > Delete Account
- Contact us for any other privacy requests

## Children

Digest is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change. Significant updates will be announced in-app.

## Contact

For privacy requests, email **digest.app.support@gmail.com**.
